Free and open

The Topmarks and Muli fonts are released under the Open Font License (OFL).
This license allows these fonts to be used freely, by an unlimited numbers of children and their teachers, for all sorts of uses. The Topmarks and Muli fonts can be used for printing, for setting digital documents, used as a webfont, and embedded in documents or in other software apps. Also, being Free Software, the fonts can be freely shared, given away or even altered.
