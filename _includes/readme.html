The Oxygen fonts are a free typeface family developed for use with the KDE desktop and GNU+Linux systems in general to extend the choice of UI type families available for Free Software desktops and devices.

The Oxygen Fonts are published under the Open Font License and the Gnu Public License.

These licenses ensure that the fonts are freely available to all.
