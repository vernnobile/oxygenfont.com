The Oxygen fonts are a free typeface family developed for use with the KDE desktop and GNU+Linux systems in general. The aim is to extend the choice of UI type families available for Free Software desktops and devices.

The Oxygen fonts are being developed in various weights and includes the development of 'Oxygen Mono', a monospaced font for use in terminals and for coding.

The Oxygen Fonts are published under the Open Font License and the Gnu Public License. These licenses ensure that the fonts are freely available to all.
