About Oxygen Font

Oxygen Font is a new typeface family being developed for use with the KDE desktop and other GNU/Linux user interfaces.

A new font for Free Desktops and GUIs on phones, tablets, laptops, workstations and TV's.

The aim of designing the Oxygen fonts is to create a new, high quality font family for the GNU/Linux communities and to expand the choice of fonts suitable for use with Free Software desktops and graphical interfaces.

The Regular and Bold weights, and the regular Monospace version, are all in a functional state, so please use them, test and feedback.

Why is it Free?

A Free font is able to be used by anyone, on any device, without being stopped pay-walls or restrictive licenses.

The Oxygen fonts are designed to be used and shared without restriction, for any purpose.

The fonts are published under both the Open Font License and the Gnu Public License. These licenses ensure that the fonts can be used, shared, and altered freely, by everyone.

The fonts can be downloaded, printed in commercial and non-commercial works, served as webfonts, and embedded in documents, software and hardware.
