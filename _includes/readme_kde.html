KDE and Oxygen Project

The initial inspiration for the development of Oxygen Fonts was the idea of an integral font family for the Oxygen theme of the KDE desktop environment.

functional, clean, uncluttered...

Taking it's cue from the Oxygen theme, the design for the Oxygen fonts aimed to make a font that was clean, practical and focussed, with a twist of elegance. The weight of the regular Oxygen font is slightly lighter than is generally found in UI fonts, to help achieve this look and feel.
